# Board Resolution Drafting Skill
## 1. Trigger Context
Activate when drafting board resolutions, written consents, or minutes of meetings.
## 2. Formatting & Structure
- **Recitals:** Draft introductory "WHEREAS" clauses explaining the context of the proposed action.
- **Resolutions:** Draft explicit "RESOLVED" clauses detailing the authorized actions.
- **Officer Authorization:** Explicitly name and authorize specific officers to execute agreements and documents on behalf of the company.
- **Counterparts & Signatures:** Include signature blocks for all directors to sign in counterparts.
## 3. Operational Rules
- For written consents, ensure resolutions are drafted as a unanimous consent unless state law allows otherwise.
- Keep records clear and concise to document corporate compliance and formal actions.
