# Statement of Work Drafting Skill
## 1. Trigger Context
Activate when drafting a Statement of Work, project schedule, or addendum under an existing MSA.
## 2. Formatting & Structure
- **MSA Integration:** Reference the date and parties of the governing MSA.
- **Scope of Work:** Detail the specific services, tasks, and project phases.
- **Deliverables & Milestones:** List all deliverables with their target completion dates.
- **Fees & Payment Schedule:** Specify the fee structure (e.g., fixed fee, hourly rates, or milestone payments) and invoicing events.
## 3. Operational Rules
- Clearly define criteria for deliverable acceptance and the review period.
- Avoid vague descriptions of scope to reduce the risk of scope creep or disputes.
