# Articles of Incorporation Drafting Skill
## 1. Trigger Context
Activate when drafting a Certificate of Incorporation, charter, or organizational filing.
## 2. Formatting & Structure
- **Entity Identity:** Include the corporate name with a required designator (e.g., "Inc." or "Corp.").
- **Corporate Purpose:** Use standard statutory language (e.g., "to engage in any lawful act or activity...").
- **Authorized Shares:** Define the total authorized capital stock, par value, and classes of shares.
- **Registered Agent:** List the physical address and name of the registered agent within the state of formation.
## 3. Operational Rules
- Use Delaware General Corporation Law (DGCL) standards as the default unless another state is specified.
- Place incorporator signature lines at the end of the document.
