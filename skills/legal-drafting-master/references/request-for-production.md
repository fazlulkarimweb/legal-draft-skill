# Request for Production Drafting Skill
## 1. Trigger Context
Activate when drafting discovery requests for documents, electronically stored information (ESI), or physical evidence.
## 2. Formatting & Structure
- **Definitions:** Include definitions for terms like "ESI" and "Metadata" to ensure comprehensive document production.
- **Categorized Requests:** Group document requests by subject matter (e.g., financial records, internal communications, or employment files).
- **Instructions:** Define the required format for producing electronic documents and metadata.
## 3. Operational Rules
- Ensure requests are relevant to the claims and proportional to the needs of the case under FRCP Rule 26(b).
- Avoid overly broad categories; define specific, clear classes of requested records.
