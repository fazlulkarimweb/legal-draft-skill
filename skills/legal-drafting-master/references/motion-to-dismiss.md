# Motion to Dismiss Drafting Skill
## 1. Trigger Context
Activate when challenging a complaint's legal sufficiency or filing procedural objections.
## 2. Formatting & Structure
- **Caption Block:** Use standard litigation caption formatting.
- **Notice of Motion:** Clearly state the date, time, courthouse location, and grounds for the motion.
- **Legal Standard:** Cite FRCP Rule 12(b)(6) or the state equivalent, establishing the legal threshold for facial insufficiency.
- **Arguments:** Structure arguments systematically using the IRAC (Issue, Rule, Application, Conclusion) framework.
- **Conclusion & Relief:** Reiterate the request that the court dismiss the action with prejudice.
## 3. Operational Rules
- Focus arguments solely on the face of the complaint and any incorporated documents.
- Clearly present each argument against the complaint's legal elements.
