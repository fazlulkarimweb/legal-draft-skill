# Last Will and Testament Drafting Skill
## 1. Trigger Context
Activate when generating a Last Will and Testament or Codicil.
## 2. Formatting & Structure
- **Exordium Clause:** Declare the testator's identity, marital status, and legal residency.
- **Revocation:** Explicitly revoke all prior wills and codicils.
- **Fiduciary Appointments:** Name executors, trustees, and guardians for minor children, along with successor appointments.
- **Bequests:** Draft separate provisions for specific bequests and the distribution of the residuary estate.
- **Witness & Notary Blocks:** Include attestation clauses for at least two witnesses and a self-proving affidavit.
## 3. Operational Rules
- Check and follow state-specific execution requirements, such as witness counts and notarization rules.
- Use complete legal names for all beneficiaries and fiduciaries; avoid ambiguous descriptions.
