# Settlement Agreement & Release Drafting Skill
## 1. Trigger Context
Activate when drafting an agreement to resolve an active dispute or threat of litigation.
## 2. Formatting & Structure
- **Preamble & Recitals:** Identify the parties, state the nature of the dispute, and declare the intent to compromise claims.
- **Settlement Terms:** Specify payment amounts, delivery methods, schedules, and any tax allocations.
- **Release of Claims:** Draft clear mutual releases. For California disputes, incorporate a waiver of Civil Code Section 1542.
- **Confidentiality:** Explicitly state the scope of confidentiality and any permitted exceptions.
- **Choice of Law & Venue:** Define the governing law and designated forum for resolving future disputes.
## 3. Operational Rules
- Clearly state that the settlement is a compromise of disputed claims and does not constitute an admission of liability.
- Ensure all financial terms preserve currency symbols exactly as negotiated.
