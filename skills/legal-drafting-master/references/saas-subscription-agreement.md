# SaaS Subscription Agreement Drafting Skill
## 1. Trigger Context
Activate when drafting SaaS subscription agreements, EULAs, or Terms of Service.
## 2. Formatting & Structure
- **SaaS License Grant:** Draft a non-exclusive, non-transferable, and revocable right to access and use the software.
- **Usage Restrictions:** List prohibited activities, such as reverse engineering, unauthorized sharing, or scraping.
- **Data Security & Privacy:** Reference compliance with data protection laws (e.g., GDPR, CCPA) and the inclusion of a Data Protection Addendum (DPA).
- **Uptime SLA & Support:** Outline service availability commitments and remedies for downtime.
## 3. Operational Rules
- Set the general liability limit to the fees paid by the customer in the preceding 12-month period.
- Ensure all dates are normalized to YYYY-MM-DD.
