# Corporate Bylaws Drafting Skill
## 1. Trigger Context
Activate when drafting bylaws for a newly formed or reorganizing corporation.
## 2. Formatting & Structure
- **Shareholder Meetings:** Outline procedures for annual and special meetings, notice requirements, and quorum thresholds.
- **Board of Directors:** Specify board size, election terms, committee delegation, and meeting protocols.
- **Officers:** List corporate officer roles (e.g., President, Treasurer, Secretary) and their duties.
- **Indemnification:** Draft mandatory indemnification clauses for directors and officers to the fullest extent permitted by state law.
## 3. Operational Rules
- Ensure consistency with the Articles of Incorporation.
- Set default quorum requirements to a majority of directors or outstanding shares unless local statutes dictate otherwise.
