# Cease & Desist Letter Drafting Skill
## 1. Trigger Context
Activate when preparing enforcement letters regarding intellectual property infringement or contract breaches.
## 2. Formatting & Structure
- **Header:** Standard professional letter format.
- **Rights Assertion:** Identify the Client's intellectual property, including patent numbers or registration details.
- **Infringement Allegations:** Detail the recipient's unauthorized activity and reference specific evidence.
- **Demands:** List clear demands, such as halting unauthorized use, preserving evidence, and providing written compliance.
- **Response Deadline:** Set a clear deadline for a formal response.
## 3. Operational Rules
- State that the letter is for settlement purposes only and is sent without prejudice to the Client's legal remedies.
- Use a firm, professional, and non-threatening tone.
