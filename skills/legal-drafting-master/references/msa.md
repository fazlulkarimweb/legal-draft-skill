# Master Services Agreement Drafting Skill
## 1. Trigger Context
Activate when establishing the general legal terms for ongoing professional services.
## 2. Formatting & Structure
- **Framework Structure:** Clearly state that specific services will be defined in subsequent Statements of Work (SOWs).
- **Payment & Invoicing:** Outline billing procedures, payment terms (e.g., Net 30), and late payment interest fees.
- **Intellectual Property Rights:** Detail ownership of pre-existing intellectual property and deliverable work product.
- **Indemnification & Liability:** Include standard mutual indemnification clauses and liability caps, with explicit carve-outs for breaches of confidentiality or gross negligence.
## 3. Operational Rules
- Balance risk allocation to avoid terms that are heavily favorable to one party, which can slow down negotiations.
- Propose Delaware or New York law as the default for governing law and jurisdiction.
