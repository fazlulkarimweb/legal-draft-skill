# Revocable Living Trust Drafting Skill
## 1. Trigger Context
Activate when asked to draft a revocable living trust or trust agreement.
## 2. Formatting & Structure
- **Declaration of Trust:** Define the grantor, initial trustee, and trust name.
- **Lifetime Administration:** Detail how trust income and principal are managed and distributed during the grantor's life.
- **Successor Trustees:** Detail the succession plan and outline the process for a successor trustee to assume office.
- **Post-Death Distribution:** Specify the distribution of trust assets or the creation of sub-trusts upon the grantor's death.
- **Trustee Powers:** Include administrative powers under state law to facilitate trust management.
## 3. Operational Rules
- Clearly state that the trust is revocable and amendable by the grantor during their lifetime.
- Include a reminder to the client that assets must be formally transferred (funded) into the trust to be governed by its terms.
