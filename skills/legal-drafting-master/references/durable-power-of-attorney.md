# Durable Power of Attorney Drafting Skill
## 1. Trigger Context
Activate when asked to draft a financial, business, or durable power of attorney.
## 2. Formatting & Structure
- **Grant of Power:** List the specific financial, banking, tax, and legal transactions the agent is authorized to conduct.
- **Durability Clause:** Include clear statutory language stating that the power survives the principal's subsequent incapacity.
- **Successor Agents:** Name successor agents to act if the primary agent is unable or unwilling to serve.
- **Execution Block:** Include witness signatures and a notary acknowledgment to ensure validity.
## 3. Operational Rules
- Follow the Uniform Power of Attorney Act (UPOAA) guidelines or state-specific statutory language.
- Draft the document as a "springing" power (effective only upon certified incapacity) if requested by the client.
