# Written Interrogatories Drafting Skill
## 1. Trigger Context
Activate when preparing discovery requests during litigation.
## 2. Formatting & Structure
- **Pleading Header:** Standard caption block at the top of the page.
- **Definitions & Instructions:** Define terms such as "Document," "Communication," and "Incident" to prevent ambiguous interpretations.
- **Interrogatories:** List numbered inquiries clearly and concisely, focusing each on a single topic.
- **Verifications & Signature:** Include signature lines for both the attorney and the responding party's verification.
## 3. Operational Rules
- Limit the total number of interrogatories to meet jurisdictional limits (e.g., 25 under FRCP Rule 33).
- Focus inquiries directly on facts, contentions, and the location of relevant documents.
