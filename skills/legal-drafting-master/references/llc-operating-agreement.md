# LLC Operating Agreement Drafting Skill
## 1. Trigger Context
Activate when asked to draft a member-managed or manager-managed LLC Operating Agreement.
## 2. Formatting & Structure
- **Capital Contributions:** Outline initial capital amounts and procedures for subsequent capital calls.
- **Allocations & Distributions:** Define how profits, losses, and cash distributions are allocated among members.
- **Management Structure:** Specify whether the LLC is member-managed or manager-managed, and define voting rights.
- **Transfer Restrictions:** Include "Right of First Refusal" (ROFR) and transfer protocols to control ownership changes.
## 3. Operational Rules
- Clearly state the tax classification of the LLC (e.g., partnership, S-corporation, or disregarded entity).
- Highlight if the state's default rules are being modified by the agreement.
