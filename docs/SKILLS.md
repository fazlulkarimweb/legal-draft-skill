# Detailed Skills Documentation

This document provides a detailed overview of the legal drafting skills available in this repository. Each skill is designed to provide specialized guidance for specific legal drafting tasks.

## 🛠 Using These Skills

### Gemini CLI
Activate in your session using:
`activate_skill(name="skill-name")`

### Claude (Claude.ai)
Use **Claude Projects**. Upload the `SKILL.md` and related files to the "Knowledge" section and paste instructions into "Custom Instructions".

### ChatGPT (GPT-4/o)
Create a **Custom GPT**. Upload the `SKILL.md` to "Knowledge" and paste the instructions into the GPT configuration.

### Manual Injection
Copy and paste the `SKILL.md` content directly into your prompt to provide the necessary drafting context to any LLM.

## Skill List

### Advance Healthcare Directive
**Description:** Drafts medical proxy designations and treatment preferences.
**Key Features:**
- Healthcare Agent Designation
- End-of-Life Instructions
- HIPAA Disclosure
- Execution Requirements

### Affidavit & Witness Declaration
**Description:** Drafts formal sworn statements for evidentiary use.
**Key Features:**
- Affirmation of Truth
- Personal Knowledge Requirement
- Factual Specificity
- Jurat/Acknowledgment

### Answer & Affirmative Defenses
**Description:** Drafts responsive pleadings and legal affirmative defenses.
**Key Features:**
- Admission/Denial Framework
- Affirmative Defense Listing
- Prayer for Relief
- Verification

### Articles of Incorporation
**Description:** Drafts foundational corporate charter and organizational filings.
**Key Features:**
- Corporate Purpose
- Stock Authorization
- Registered Agent Designation
- Incorporator Information

### Board Resolution
**Description:** Drafts formal authorizations and board-level decisions.
**Key Features:**
- Recitals (Whereas clauses)
- Resolved Clauses
- Secretary's Certification
- Effective Date

### Cease and Desist
**Description:** Drafts formal demands to halt unauthorized activities.
**Key Features:**
- Identification of Infringement
- Demand for Action
- Deadline for Compliance
- Reservation of Rights

### Civil Complaint
**Description:** Drafts formal initial pleadings to initiate a lawsuit.
**Key Features:**
- Jurisdictional Statement
- Factual Allegations
- Causes of Action
- Demand for Judgment

### Corporate Bylaws
**Description:** Drafts internal corporate rules and governance frameworks.
**Key Features:**
- Shareholder Meeting Rules
- Board Composition & Duties
- Officer Roles
- Indemnification Clauses

### Durable Power of Attorney
**Description:** Drafts authorizations for financial and business management.
**Key Features:**
- General vs. Limited Powers
- Durability Clause
- Successor Agents
- Statutory Compliance

### Last Will and Testament
**Description:** Drafts testamentary documents for asset distribution.
**Key Features:**
- Executor Appointment
- Specific Bequests
- Residuary Estate Distribution
- Guardianship Provisions

### Legal Memorandum
**Description:** Enforce memorandum header labeling, active voice in background sections, and citation mapping.
**Key Features:**
- Header Labeling
- Active Voice Backgrounds
- Signatory Requirements
- Sequential Citation Mapping

### LLC Operating Agreement
**Description:** Defines ownership, management, and LLC tax structures.
**Key Features:**
- Member Contributions
- Allocation of Profits/Losses
- Management Structure
- Dissolution Procedures

### Motion for Summary Judgment
**Description:** Drafts motions for judgment based on undisputed facts.
**Key Features:**
- Statement of Undisputed Facts
- Legal Standard Application
- Argument Section
- Conclusion/Relief Requested

### Motion to Dismiss
**Description:** Drafts motions challenging a complaint's legal sufficiency.
**Key Features:**
- Procedural Background
- Rule 12(b)(6) (or equivalent) Analysis
- Failure to State a Claim Argument
- Request for Dismissal with Prejudice

### Master Service Agreement (MSA)
**Description:** Drafts primary frameworks for ongoing professional services.
**Key Features:**
- Term & Termination
- Intellectual Property Rights
- Confidentiality Obligations
- Limitation of Liability

### Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA)
**Description:** Drafts mutual agreements to protect confidential information.
**Key Features:**
- Definition of Confidential Information
- Permitted Use
- Return/Destruction of Materials
- Equitable Relief Clause

### Patent Claims
**Description:** Drafts utility patent claims following strict USPTO-style guidelines.
**Key Features:**
- Preamble
- Transitional Phrases
- Body & Limitations
- Dependent vs. Independent Claims

### Request for Production
**Description:** Drafts formal requests for physical or electronic evidence.
**Key Features:**
- Definitions & Instructions
- Categorized Document Requests
- Time/Place of Production
- ESI Specifications

### Revocable Living Trust
**Description:** Drafts trust structures for asset management and distribution.
**Key Features:**
- Trust Property Identification
- Beneficiary Designations
- Trustee Powers & Duties
- Revocation/Amendment Terms

### SaaS Subscription Agreement
**Description:** Drafts terms for accessing cloud-hosted software services.
**Key Features:**
- Service Level Agreements (SLA)
- Data Security & Privacy
- User Seat Limitations
- Subscription Tiers & Billing

### Settlement Agreement
**Description:** Drafts binding agreements to resolve legal disputes.
**Key Features:**
- Mutual Release of Claims
- Payment Terms
- Non-Disparagement Clause
- Confidentiality of Terms

### Statement of Work (SOW)
**Description:** Defines specific project deliverables, timelines, and pricing.
**Key Features:**
- Deliverable Descriptions
- Project Milestones
- Acceptance Criteria
- Resource Requirements

### Trademark Description
**Description:** Drafts identifications of mark-related goods and services.
**Key Features:**
- Class Identification
- Precise Goods/Services Wording
- International Class Mapping
- Usage Examples

### Written Interrogatories
**Description:** Drafts formal written questions for discovery purposes.
**Key Features:**
- Prefatory Instructions
- Interrogatory Numbering
- Verification Block
- Relevance Constraints
